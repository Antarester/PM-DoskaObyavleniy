Роль: Financial Controller
This role defines financial control points and measurements for the portfolio in alignment with the organization's strategy.
Наборы ролей: Portfolio Management
Взаимосвязи
Основное описание

This role is responsible for supervising accounting and financial reporting activities in a organization. It may also be responsible for advising on potential acquisitions and business decisions made by an organization by providing regular and relevant analysis of financial data that approve business decisions or influence changing them.

Персонал
Подходы к назначениюA person performing this role is typically an experienced finance or accounting professional who reports to the organization's chief financial officer.
Ключевые условия
In the context of portfolio management, this role supports the identification of which components are worth pursuing and which ones are not, based on financial investments and expected return.
