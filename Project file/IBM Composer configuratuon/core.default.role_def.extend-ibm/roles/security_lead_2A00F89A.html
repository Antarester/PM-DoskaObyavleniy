Роль: Security Lead
This role establishes the processes and internal standards for ensuring the security
of the systems deployed and developed within an organization.
Наборы ролей: Software Capability Improvement
Взаимосвязи
Основное описание
Many organizations have an internal Security team that is responsible for protecting their IT systems and data from malicious attacks and ensuring that the organization is not exposed to a security risk. Each Security team typically has a Security Lead who is responsible for establishing the processes and standards for ensuring the security of the IT systems that they are responsible for.
Персонал
Квалификация

A person filling this role should have these skills:

  • Deep knowledge of security testing approaches and techniques
  • Very good understanding of the industry standards and regulations
  • Intimate knowledge of the types of systems or applications under test, or both
  • Knowledge of the networking architecture
  • Very good knowledge of the test automated security tools 
Подходы к назначениюThis role is typically held by an individual with many years of experience as a security professional who has a deep understanding of the current security trends.
