Роль: Portfolio Review Board
This role reviews and prioritizes implementation of portfolio components.
Наборы ролей: Portfolio Management
Взаимосвязи
Основное описание

This role is central to reviewing and approving decisions, such as making investments on product proposals, and replacing or retiring existing products. This role accesses organizational strategic information, such as ideas, product descriptions, product roadmaps, customer-sensitive information, competitors information, and so on to propose changes to organizational business objectives as necessary.

This role also reviews selected components to be part of the portfolio, and prioritizes which components will be pursued or developed in which order.

Персонал
Подходы к назначению

This role is performed by a group of professionals in senior positions, typically including people playing other roles, including but not limited to: portfolio analysts, portfolio managers, product managers, and project managers.
