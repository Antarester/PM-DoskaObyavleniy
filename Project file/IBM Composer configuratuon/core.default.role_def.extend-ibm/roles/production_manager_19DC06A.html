Роль: Production Manager
This role is responsible for managing work and resources related to the development or manufacturing of products.
Наборы ролей: Portfolio Management
Взаимосвязи
Основное описание

This role is responsible for planning and coordinating work activities and resources necessary for developing or manufacturing products. This role is responsible for implementing production tracking and control systems for cost and quality of products, which may take into consideration the existing production systems, past cost estimates, and organizational policies.

Ключевые условия
In the context of portfolio management, this role participates in the decision of how to allocate resources supply to work on portfolio components demand.
