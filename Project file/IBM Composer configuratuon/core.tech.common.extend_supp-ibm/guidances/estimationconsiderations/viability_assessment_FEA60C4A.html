Рекомендации по оценке: Viability Assessment
The time taken to perform an initial viability assessment can be anything from one to 15 person days depending on a number of factors such as project size, complexity, formality of the assessment etc.

Основное описание

The effort required for creating the Viability Assessment depends on several factors:

  • The extent of technical risk on the project (it is worth investing more effort in frequent and thorough viability assessments on high risk projects than where technical risk is low)
  • The ease and speed with which the reviewers can become familiar with all relevant documentation
  • The size and complexity of the project.

On a small project, two people may conduct a viability assessment in half a day each (i.e., 1 person day effort).  On a large project, external assessors in a workshop format often perform a viability assessment.  The effort can be estimated from the number of attendees, and the duration of the workshop.  For example, 5 people for a 2-day workshop, with another day preparation and follow up, i.e., 15 effort days.

For estimation purposes, the effort required for assessing viability and updating the Viability Assessment can sometimes be combined with other review effort at each project checkpoint.
