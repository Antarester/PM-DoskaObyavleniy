Инструмент: IBM Rational Requirements Composer
IBM Rational Requirements Composer enables business analysts and software development teams to define requirements using a variety of techniques on a collaborative platform. With Requirements Composer, you can elicit, analyze, specify, and validate different levels of requirements. Its integration with Rational RequisitePro provides an overall solution for requirements definition and management.
