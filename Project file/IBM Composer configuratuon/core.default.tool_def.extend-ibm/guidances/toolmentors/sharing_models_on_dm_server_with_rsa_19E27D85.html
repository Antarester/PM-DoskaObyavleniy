Основное описание

Architectures, models, and designs help teams address the growing complexity of today's software and systems. To integrate design activities into the collaborative lifecycle, you can share models on the Design Management Server. There are two approaches to managing design information:

  • Design resources can be externally managed and imported on demand
  • Design resources can be actively managed by the Design Management Server

The following topics are useful to share models on the Rational Software Architect Design Management Server:

  • Importing RSA models to the Design Management Server
  • Share models and designs to collaborate with stakeholders
  • Exploring shared models and designs
